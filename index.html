I am currently Chief Technical Officer at WayPaver Foundation - a research-based grant making institution that supports science and technology development to enable permanent lunar settlement. One of my main areas of work is the Lunar Settlement Index - a catalog of all the roadblocks preventing us from establishing a lunar settlement. I lead this project along with most of WayPaver's technical projects and partnerships to push forward the cause globally and help create a lunar settlement within the coming decades.
